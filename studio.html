Our Vision

HP_Architects was founded in February 2018 with the goal of fostering effective and responsive design collaborations in the building and environment fields. The founder and principal architect, Ar. Himanshu Parchani, uses his extensive knowledge of design and crafting to guide his approach to projects and shape his architectural style.

In just three years, HP Architects has already completed over 50 projects in more than 10 cities across Chhattisgarh and is rapidly expanding. The firm specialises in interior design, architecture, site development, and is now exploring turnkey projects.

As architects, HP Architects believes that our surroundings have a significant impact on the quality of our lives, whether it be in the workplace or at home. As such, they view architecture as a tool to shape our environment and improve our lives. Their design philosophy is based on the principle of "Design, Detail, Deliver," which leads to successful and unique projects.

The firm's goal is to design and build spaces that are functional, aesthetically pleasing, and comfortable for their clients. Currently, HP Architects offers services in architecture and interior design, as well as master planning.

HP architects have grown exponentially by accepting every challenge that came it's way. Our initial success came from designing high rises in Chattisgarh, our base.
